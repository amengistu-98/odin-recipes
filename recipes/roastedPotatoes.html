Roasted Potatoes

Ingredients

  • Yukon Gold potatoes (or potatoes of your choice)
  • 1/4 cup melted duck fat or chicken fat (you can choose a cooking fat of your choice; lard and olive oil are also good for this recipe)
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground onion or onion flakes
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes in halves or quarters so they are all even-sized.
  2. Bring a pot of water or broth to a boil and parboil the potatoes until they can easily be pierced with a fork (about 10-12 minutes).
  3. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit (or 230 degrees Celsius) while potatoes are boiling.
  4. Once potatoes are parboiled, put them in a large boil and add cornstarch, duck fat, salt, and your seasonings and toss them until the potatoes are combined.
  5. Spread potatoes in a baking tray or sheet that is lined with parchment paper, making sure that they don't touch each other.
  6. Roast the potatoes in oven for 40 to 50 minutes until crispy, making sure to toss them half way through cooking to promote even browning on all sides of the chopped potatoes.
