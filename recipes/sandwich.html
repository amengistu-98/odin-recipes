Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (I use whole wheat)
  • Ham slices
  • 1 slice cheese (I prefer American Cheese, but choose a melting cheese of your choice
  • 2 tbsp butter (make sure it is soft and at room temperature to be spreadable)

Steps

  1. Put cheese and ham in between two pieces of bread to form the sandwich.
  2. Spread butter on both sides of the sandwich.
  3. Toast both sides of the ham and cheese sandwich on a pan or griddle.
  4. Once both sides of the sandwich is crispy and the cheese in the sandwich is melted, your sandwich is ready to serve and eat!
